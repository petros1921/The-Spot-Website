About The Spot

The Spot is where fine dining, music, and nightlife meet in one refined space. Located in the heart of Bole, we create an atmosphere that blends modern elegance with vibrant entertainment.

From live acoustic performances to energetic DJ nights, every experience at The Spot is carefully curated to bring people together over great food, handcrafted cocktails, and unforgettable moments.
